By the people for the people: River

River, lead by Martha Wilcox and Moire Lendering was a temporary mosaic created in the summer of 2015 by engaging the community in painting river rocks in various colors of blue, green and purple. It was intended to stimulate dialogue about water usage in our very arid environment.
