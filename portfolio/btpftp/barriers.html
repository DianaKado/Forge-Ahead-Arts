By the people for the people: Barriers

In summer 2016 we installed Barriers on the fence by the old Tresieras market on Main Street. We spent out 10 weeks at various locations asking residents to share their stories about barriers in their lives. We also posted all the responses our website, so although the piece itself was only up for about 4 weeks, it lives on, and we have received some comments on it after the fact. Diana Kado was the lead artist and she is considering ways to continue and build on this piece.
