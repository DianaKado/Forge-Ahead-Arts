By the people for the people: Once Upon a Yarn

Once Upon a Yarn, installed at the Newhall library for 2 months in 2016, was the brainchild of Ann Mukherjee. The central pieces created for this installation represented different children’s books. We have saved them and would like to create either a smaller traveling installation out of them or possibly some flat pieces that could be donated to the children’s wing of the hospital.
