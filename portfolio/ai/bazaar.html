Artist Installations: Bazaar

Bazaar was a collaboration by Martha Wilcox and Moire Lendering, installed in the summer of 2015 at the Westfield Valencia City Art Space. By creating an allusion to 3rd world marketplace, using materials iconic of the American market, the piece asked the viewer to consider the continuum of economic exchange.
