Participatory Events: Apple Pie

Moire Lendering created this pieace to raise questions about how we as American’s view our place in the world. The audience at the ArtSlam engaged with the piece by eating a piece of apple pie, which was served on flag plates, made in China, and with “serviettes” created by folding the front pages of newspapers around the world.
