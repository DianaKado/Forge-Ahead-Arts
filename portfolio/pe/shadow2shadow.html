Participatory Events: Shadow to Shadow

For the October 2017 SENSES block party, Forge Ahead Arts created a shadow booth. Participants drew prompts from a box, then struck a pose to which their partner reacted, not knowing what the prompt was. Shadows of over 40 pairs of people were created and photos of these poses were tweeted @forgearts #shadow2shadow.
