Participatory Events: Sweet Shop

Moire Lendering and Stephanie O'Connor fashioned small hands out of chocolate for the audience to consume. The “price” of each piece was the picture of child working in the cocoa fields, which the audience then hung to create a fluttering display. This piece, created for Chocolate Slam in Sept. 2014 aimed to raise awareness of the issue of child trafficking in the chocolate industry.
