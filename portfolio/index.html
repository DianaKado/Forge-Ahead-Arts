We create art installations and events that stimulate creativity
and dialogue in Santa Clarita. Undoubtedly it will continue to
evolve, but for now, our work falls falls into 3 broad categories:

by the people for the people

The first category are participatory public art works that are designed and led by an artist, but created by members of the community. So far we have completed 5 of these projects.

participatory events

Our second category of programing includes participatory installations and events. Although there is some overlap, these are different from the “by the people for the people” pieces in the way that the community participates.

artist installations

Finally we have also produced two installations that are not participatory. We hope to build on this kind of programing with additional pieces as well as continuing to expand the circle of artists with whom we are working.
